Education is one of the most important and powerful thing you can use to change the world. With the use of education, you will gain knowledge and lessons that you can use in this life. Giving you the wisdom that you need that to help you to survive life. School is the place where everything started, in this place you will gain knowledge, lessons and experiences.

As days pass by when the school start when the school started, we notice same thing wrong because we also experienced it firsthand. The lack of facilities in the university is really a problem. This school accepts a lot of students without enough rooms to accommodate them. This causes a lot of problems because of this

In Bulacan State University we experienced lack of classrooms and that is that most problems of every student in the campus. Through the course of learning a well-good facilities and service is needed in order to fulfill the satisfactions of all studnets.

We always have to roam around the campus to look for available spots where we can seat and it’s not just timing but also uncomfortable because we can’t do our school works properly without a permanent room. And as seniors of the high school department we don’t want our younger ones to also experience this hardship that we did so Jimuel and friends decided to organize a movement to let the university’s President know your concerns. We believe that learning is fun when you’re not studying campaign aims to gather support from the students around the campus tell to higher ups that we need more rooms so we can study comfortably and be more productive. You can reach us and participate through the digital space that we created. They can say here every concerns they have regarding the problems they encounter in our daily lives insides the campus.
